isletoken

isletoken is a cultural access marker defined in the IsleToken Triple Anchor Model (UKCS deposited).

It is anchored in creative authorship, an evolving corpus of works, and legally recognized creative time. It does not represent ownership, financial value, or participation in any market or speculative system.

The term “isletoken” as used here is not associated with any financial or market-traded token of the same or similar name.
