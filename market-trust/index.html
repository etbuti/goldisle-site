What it is / 这是什么

Simple declaration, formal record.

本服务用于记录商户在市场节点上的自申报出货信息，包括身份、时间、地点与节点签发记录。

This service records merchant self-declarations at a market node, including identity, time, place, and node-issued evidence.

核心边界 / Core Boundary

这不是“真假担保”，而是“正规记录”。

This is not a guarantee of factual truth, but a formal record of declaration.

How it works / 怎么做

三步完成记录

01

插入身份U盘

系统读取商户身份，不需要复杂登录。

02

填写商品与数量

只需填写最少信息，简单明了。

03

生成正式记录页

自动生成编号、时间、签发记录与可查看页面。

Notice / 重要说明

本证明记录的是商户基于其自身身份介质完成的自申报内容，以及节点系统对该申报的时间、地点与签发记录。除非另行升级至增强审计流程，本证明不独立验证货物数量、质量或外部事实真伪。

This certificate records the merchant’s self-declaration made through its registered identity medium, together with the node-issued time, place, and issuance record. Unless separately upgraded to an enhanced audit process, this certificate does not independently verify the physical quantity, quality, or external factual truth of the goods.
