Text

At night, the city does not disappear. It exhales. Movement becomes low frequency, and light loses its urgency.

“Night City” is not an overview. It is a listening position — somewhere between inside and outside, awake but unrequired.

In Isle of Not Alone, this node represents time released from purpose, and sound allowed to exist without explanation.
