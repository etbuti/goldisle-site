Settlement Notice (B2B)

Payments processed under this framework are accepted solely for procurement execution and settlement services. GOLDISLE LIGHT ORG does not sell goods, does not act as a commodity seller, and does not take ownership of products at any stage.

Delivery is performed by the manufacturer under FOB Qingdao Port (Incoterms®). International freight and insurance are arranged by the Buyer (or the Buyer’s nominated forwarder).

Option A — PayPal (Business Invoice)

PayPal is used as a settlement channel for procurement execution services. To preserve a clear audit trail, we issue invoices (B2B) rather than collecting payments as retail transactions.
