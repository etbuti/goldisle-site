Book Project

Sound as Event: Time, Decision, and Embodied Execution in Sound-Based Practice
Research monograph in preparation. Proposal completed; publisher inquiries in progress.

Concept

The book develops a conceptual ground for understanding sound as an occurrence rather than an object, integrating applied studies on breath, pulse, and voice as interrelated conditions of sound occurrence.

Proposed Structure (High-Level)

  • Part I · Ground — Sound objects to sound events; event, time, and decision
  • Part II · Conditions — Breath, Pulse, Voice
  • Part III · Integration — Non-representation; algorithmic thinking beyond output
  • Part IV · Applications — Practice as event practice; listening as an event
