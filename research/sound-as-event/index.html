Sound as Event

Research Hub — Evan Bei · Independent Scholar

Sound as Event is a long-term research project that approaches sound not as a fixed object or representational medium, but as a temporally situated event shaped by decision, embodiment, and contextual conditions.

Applied Studies

  • Breath as Event — embodied conditions of initiation (whether sound may occur)
  • Pulse as Event — temporal decision structures (when sound may occur)
  • Voice as Event — execution and presence (who/what performs the sound)
