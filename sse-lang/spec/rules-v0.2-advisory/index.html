SSE-Lang — Advisory Rules v0.2 (Public Demo)

These rules are soft flags designed to report risk conditions derived from Rating.* inputs. They do not override the hard infeasibility verdict in SSE-Lang v0.1.

Rule List

IDIFTHENStatusLevel
V0_2_A1 AnyCriticalFlag = true Advisory: critical rating present draftsoft

Notes

Soft rules are evaluated in the same matching engine as hard rules, but they are separated by level: "soft". The endpoint may display a YES (ADVISORY) verdict when soft rules are triggered while no hard infeasibility is triggered.
