What is SSE-Lang? 什么是 SSE-Lang？ EN SSE-Lang (Structured Safety & Evaluation Language) is a lightweight rule-based language for expressing feasibility judgement in scientific and engineering contexts where fully quantitative modeling is unavailable or inappropriate. 中 SSE-Lang（Structured Safety & Evaluation Language，结构化安全与评估语言）是一种轻量级的规则语言，用于在不适合或无法进行完整定量建模的科学与工程场景中，表达可行性判断。 ⸻ Why SSE-Lang? 为什么需要 SSE-Lang？ EN In many real-world domains, decisions are not derived from prediction alone. Expert reasoning often appears as conditional exclusions, necessary constraints, and advisory considerations that are implicit and difficult to audit. 中 在许多真实场景中，决策并非来自预测本身。专家判断往往以条件性排除、必要约束和建议性提示的形式出现，但这些推理通常是隐含的、难以审计的。 EN SSE-Lang is designed to make this reasoning explicit, structured, and reproducible—without pretending it is a predictive model. 中 SSE-Lang 的目标，是在不伪装为预测模型的前提下，将这些推理显式化、结构化并可复现。 ⸻ What SSE-Lang Is (and Is Not) SSE-Lang 是什么（以及不是什么） EN — SSE-Lang is: • A rule language, not a machine-learning model • Deterministic, interpretable, and auditable • Explicit about why a judgement is reached 中 — SSE-Lang 是： • 一种规则语言，而非机器学习模型 • 确定性的、可解释的、可审计的 • 清楚说明“为何得出该判断” EN — SSE-Lang is not: • A performance predictor • A substitute for expert judgement • A black-box decision system 中 — SSE-Lang 不是： • 性能预测器 • 专家判断的替代品 • 黑箱式决策系统 ⸻ Core Concepts 核心概念 1. Explicit Inputs 1. 显式输入 EN Inputs are declared as structured statements that are both human-readable and machine-interpretable. IonPathDimensionality: #3D; Rating.Stability: rating.moderate; CHECK; 中 输入以结构化语句形式声明，兼顾人类可读性与机器可解析性。 ⸻ 2. Derived Semantics (v0.2) 2. 派生语义（v0.2） EN Higher-level indicators are derived from multiple qualitative inputs rather than manually specified, improving consistency and traceability. 中 高层指标由多个定性输入自动派生，而非人工填写，从而提升一致性与可追溯性。 ⸻ 3. Hard vs Soft Rules 3. 硬规则与软规则 EN • Hard rules define strict infeasibility conditions. Any triggered hard infeasible rule yields NO. • Soft (advisory) rules do not veto feasibility but attach cautionary signals. 中 • 硬规则：定义严格的不可行条件，任一触发即判定 NO。 • 软规则（建议）：不否决可行性，但给出风险提示。 EN This mirrors real expert reasoning: not all warnings are vetoes. 中 这与真实专家判断一致：并非所有警告都等同于否决。 ⸻ 4. Transparent Judgement Trace 4. 透明的判断轨迹 EN Each evaluation produces a clear verdict, triggered rules, derived indicators, and a stable trace identifier suitable for citation. 中 每次评估都会生成清晰的结论、触发规则、派生指标，以及可用于引用的稳定 Trace ID。 ⸻ Why a Language, Not a Model? 为什么是语言，而不是模型？ EN Many failures stem from implicit assumptions rather than weak models. SSE-Lang makes assumptions explicit and discussable. 中 许多问题并非模型能力不足，而是隐含假设不可见。SSE-Lang 将假设显式化，使其可讨论、可审计。 Public Demonstrator (v0.2) 公共演示版（v0.2） EN The current web interface is a public demonstrator for methodological illustration and academic discussion. 中 当前网页界面为公共演示版，用于方法展示与学术讨论。 Status / 状态 SSE-Lang v0.2 — Stable (Public Demonstrator) The language core and rule structure are frozen. ⸻ Intended Use 适用范围 EN • Methodological illustration • Transparent decision logic documentation • Educational and exploratory use 中 • 方法论展示 • 透明的决策逻辑文档 • 教学与探索性使用 EN It is not intended for automated deployment without expert oversight. 中 不适用于脱离专家监督的自动化部署。 ⸻ Closing Note 结语 EN SSE-Lang does not replace expertise. It respects expert reasoning by making it legible, explicit, and discussable. 中 SSE-Lang 并不取代专家，而是通过让专家推理可读、可见、可讨论来尊重专业判断。
